It's a small shop based in California created by me, Dalia (that's my name). So basically, I've always been really crafty and enjoyed creating things since a young age.

After having a shop dedicated to bracelets and necklaces I began to venture off into making things for something I was really passionate about: music. I started making pins for bands that I liked because it was a cool way to show my love and devotion for that band in a cool and punk rock way ya feel?

In an incredible stroke of luck other people thought so too, and they not only started buying pins, they also suggested more for me to design. I got to know more music, and people who are passionate about music and that's more than I could have ver hoped more.

But yeah, that's how Bandshite grew into something way bigger than I could have ever imagined. The best part is that it's still growing, so check out our site, recommend some bands, we can't wait to hear from you!
